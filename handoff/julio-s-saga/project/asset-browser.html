Boss 01 — Abacate Ancião

"Discípulo do Bruxo Kale." A druidic avocado titan who rules the Dark Vegan Forest and wants meat erased from the nine realms. Three escalating phases + two minion types.

assets/bosses/abacate-anciao/abacate-anciao-sheet.png1313 × 1198

Phases

FASE 1 · "A Natureza Irá Vencer"
Base form. Throws pits.
FASE 2 · "O Núcleo Despertou"
Core glows, drips guacamole, stronger.
FASE 3 · "A Floresta É Minha!"
Overgrown root-titan, full power.

Attacks

  • Pit Throw
  • Binding Roots
  • Guacamole Explosion
  • Chasing Vines
  • Leaf Storm
  • Summon Allies

Minions

  • 🥕 Spear Carrot
  • 🥦 Shield Broccoli
